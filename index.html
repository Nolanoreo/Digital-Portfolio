🐠 Why Marine Biology?

Why I Love Marine Biology

The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet, yet so much of it remains unexplored and unknown. That mystery is what draws me in. From the intricate ecosystems of coral reefs to the strange creatures of the deep sea, marine biology sits at the intersection of science, discovery, and conservation — and I can't imagine anything more exciting to dedicate my life to.


Growing up near the coast, the ocean has always felt like home. I want to use science to better understand and protect it for future generations.
